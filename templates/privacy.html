Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's how we handle your data.

The Short Version

  • We don't require accounts for basic file transfers
  • We don't sell your data to anyone
  • Files are automatically deleted after the storage period (7 days for free, 30 days for Pro)
  • We use HTTPS encryption for all transfers
  • We only collect what's necessary to provide the service

Information We Collect

For Anonymous Transfers (No Account)

When you use SendFiles.Online without creating an account, we collect:

  • Uploaded files: Stored temporarily for the transfer duration (7 days)
  • IP address: Used for rate limiting and abuse prevention
  • Basic analytics: Page views, transfer counts (anonymized)

We do NOT collect your name, email, or any personal information for anonymous transfers.

For Registered Accounts

If you create an account (required for Pro features), we also collect:

  • Email address: For account management and notifications
  • Password: Stored securely using industry-standard hashing
  • Payment information: Processed by our payment partners (Stripe/PayPal) - we don't store your card details
  • Transfer history: So you can manage your uploads

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the file transfer service
  • To prevent abuse and enforce our terms of service
  • To send account-related emails (if you have an account)
  • To improve our service through anonymized analytics

We do NOT use your information for advertising or sell it to third parties.

File Storage and Deletion

  • Free transfers: Files are automatically deleted after 7 days
  • Pro transfers: Files are automatically deleted after 30 days
  • Manual deletion: You can delete your files at any time from the upload success page

When files are deleted, they are permanently removed from our servers. We do not keep backups of user files.

Data Security

  • All transfers use HTTPS (TLS 1.2+) encryption
  • Files are stored on secure servers with restricted access
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
  • We regularly update our security practices

Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Session management: To keep you logged in (if you have an account)
  • Analytics: To understand how people use our service (anonymized)

You can disable cookies in your browser, though this may affect some functionality.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Payment processing: Stripe and PayPal for secure payments
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns (anonymized data only)
  • CDN: For faster file delivery

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data (contact us for a copy)
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request correction of inaccurate data

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us.
