About SendFiles.Online

We believe sharing files should be as simple as sharing a link.

Our Mission

Every day, millions of people struggle with file size limits when trying to share photos, videos, documents, and projects. Email attachments max out. Messaging apps compress your files. Cloud services require accounts and complicated sharing settings.

We built SendFiles.Online to fix that. Drag. Drop. Share. That's it. No account required, no confusing interfaces, no waiting for uploads to process. Just fast, simple file sharing that works.

What We Offer

Fast Transfers

Our servers are optimized for speed. Upload and share files quickly, without waiting around.

Generous Free Tier

Send files up to 10GB for free. No credit card, no signup for basic transfers. Just upload and share.

Secure by Default

All transfers use HTTPS encryption. Add password protection for sensitive files.

Works Everywhere

No app to install. Works in any modern browser on desktop, mobile, or tablet.
